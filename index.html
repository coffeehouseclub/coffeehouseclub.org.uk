Coffee House Club

A place to talk & think

Coffee House Club was a discussion group held on the first Sunday of every month. We meet in the afternoon to explore & share ideas together with other intellectually curious people in a public space.

Coffee House Club is on indefinite hiatus. We may return to the format one day, but for now we'd like to thank you all for sharing with us.

We had a wonderful time making friends and exploring ideas with you all.

Founded by David, Irina & Jack.
