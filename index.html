Coffee House Club

A place to think & talk

Coffee House Club is a discussion group held on the first Sunday of every month. We meet to explore and share ideas together with other curious people in a public space.

If you'd like to join in, why not introduce yourself on the mailing list, and check out next months theme in the calendar?

Founded by David, Irina and Jack.
